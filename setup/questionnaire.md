# Onboarding Questionnaire — FootballHeritage

## Business Context
1. What is the primary revenue model? (subscription, commission on bets, freemium, other?)
2. Which jurisdictions/regulators govern this platform? What compliance requirements apply?
3. Who are the key competitors, and what differentiates FootballHeritage from them?
4. What is the target user base size and growth trajectory for the next 12 months?

## Scope & Priorities
5. Which sports are highest priority to support at launch? Which are nice-to-have?
6. What is the minimum prediction accuracy threshold for models to be considered "production ready"?
7. Are there existing integrations (payment gateways, identity providers, data vendors) that must be supported?
8. What is the target timeline for MVP vs full feature set?

## Technical Preferences
9. What is the hosting/infrastructure preference? (self-hosted, cloud VPS, Kubernetes, serverless?)
10. Are there any SLAs or uptime requirements for the prediction/odds serving APIs?
11. What monitoring and observability tools are preferred? (Datadog, Grafana, Sentry, custom?)
12. Is there a preference for open-source vs commercial tools for any part of the stack?
